Oat Porridge with Fruit Recipe

Description

This one here ain't for your friday night funzies. Sometimes you need an emergancy source of nutritiously packed healthy complex carbs. Now those buzz words wont get you salivating but they'll sure get you to where you need to go when taste isn't a factor. Before we begin, we need to understand that these bad boys come in different sizes. I prefer rolled oats as the bigger ones tend to have abit more bite to them whereas I like it somewhat like a paste but not too pasty. If you want 'em smoother go for more refined oats, if you like a bit more chew in them then try out the bigger ones.

Ingredients

Here are the things we'll need:

  • About a cup of your preferred oats
  • Water or milk
  • Banana
  • Berries of preference. I usually go for blueberres.
  • A breakfast bowl

Steps

  1. Fill a breakfast bowl halfway with oats.
  2. Pour in the water or milk until it just barely covers the oats and mix it with a spoon.
  3. Microwave it for a minute.
  4. Chop a banana into it and throw in the berries.
